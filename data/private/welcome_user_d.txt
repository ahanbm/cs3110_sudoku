
Welcome to CS3110_diagonal_sudoku!
This program allows you to play diagonal sudoku conveniently and easily in your terminal.

This game comes preloaded with several classic diagonal sudoku puzzles.
You can change the file "data/initial.csv" for a custom puzzle of your choice.
As you do this, do not change any of the files in the directory "data/private", as those 
are crucial to the game's internal functionality and should not be changed.
